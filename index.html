Shengyu Huang 黄盛宇

I am a third-year PhD student in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing Lab of ETH Zürich, jointly supervised by Konrad Schindler and Andreas Wieser. I will join Federico Tombari's team in Google Zurich as Student Researcher this summer. Previously, I was a research scientist intern with Sanja Fidler in NVIDIA Toronto AI Lab.

I obtained my master degree in Geomatics Engineering from ETH Zürich. My undergraduate study was done in Shanghai, where I spent my first year in Mathematics but changed my direction and completed a bachelor in Surveying and Mapping Engineering at Tongji University.

I co-organize the ETH Geomatics Seminar, please reach out if you are interested in presenting.
